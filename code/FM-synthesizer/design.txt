
FM. BISON synthesizer design.

Features for version 0.1:

- 1 hardcoded tone (patch).
- Polyphony of 8; no voice stealing.
- 1 global ADSR which modulates amplitude.
- 1 simple filter for cutoff and resonance.

Features for 0.2:

- Basic patch framework for different tones.
- More elaborate filter (MOOG) plus wet<->dry mix.
- Voice stealing.
