
FM. BISON synthesizer design

Features for version 0.1:

- 1 patch at a time; hardcoded for now.
- Polyphony is 8; no voice stealing algorithm *yet*.
- Patch is driven by a single carrier with 1 modulator.
- 1 global configurable ADSR modulates amplitude.
- Cosine tilt envelope modulates 
- Vorticity 0.1 (vortex shedding) modulates the amplitude.
- Global gain control.
- MOOG ladder filter with cutoff and resonance with a wet<->dry crossfade control.

Features for 0.2:
- Control over modulation index (physical, LFO or both).
- Pitch bend (physical).
